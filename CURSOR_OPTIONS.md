# üé® SMOOTH CURSOR STYLE OPTIONS

Here are 10 different cursor styles for the ProCraft website. Each option has a unique design and behavior.

---

## **OPTION 1: Classic Ring + Dot** (Current)
**Style**: Outer ring with inner dot
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 ring, #10367D dot
**Behavior**: Ring grows on hover, glow effect
**Best For**: Professional, clean look

Default: 40px ring + 8px dot
Hover: 50px ring + 10px dot (scale 1.5x)

---

## **OPTION 2: Filled Circle**
**Style**: Solid filled circle, no ring
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 background
**Behavior**: Expands on hover, blur effect
**Best For**: Modern, minimalist

Default: 12px solid circle
Hover: 24px solid circle + blur
Click: 8px compressed

---

## **OPTION 3: Crosshair**
**Style**: Plus sign crosshair
**Brand Colors**: #10367D lines
**Behavior**: Rotates on movement
**Best For**: Precision, technical feel

Default: 30px crosshair
Hover: 40px + rotate 45deg
Lines: 2px thick

---

## **OPTION 4: Double Ring**
**Style**: Two concentric rings
**Brand Colors**: Inner #10367D, Outer #74B4D9
**Behavior**: Rings scale independently
**Best For**: Layered, dynamic look

Default: 20px + 40px rings
Hover: 25px + 55px (outer grows more)
Click: Both compress

---

## **OPTION 5: Glowing Orb**
**Style**: Solid dot with large glow
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 glow, #10367D core
**Behavior**: Intense glow on hover
**Best For**: Futuristic, eye-catching

Default: 10px dot + 20px glow
Hover: 12px dot + 60px glow
Glow: box-shadow blur 30px

---

## **OPTION 6: Square + Dot**
**Style**: Rounded square ring with dot
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 square, #10367D dot
**Behavior**: Square rotates on movement
**Best For**: Unique, geometric

Default: 36px square (8px border-radius) + dot
Hover: 48px square + scale
Rotation: Continuous based on velocity

---

## **OPTION 7: Morphing Shape**
**Style**: Circle that morphs to horizontal line on hover
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9
**Behavior**: Shape transition
**Best For**: Creative, playful

Default: 16px circle
Hover: 40px x 4px horizontal pill
Smooth morph with border-radius

---

## **OPTION 8: Trail Effect**
**Style**: Cursor with following trail
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 gradient trail
**Behavior**: 3-5 delayed trail dots
**Best For**: Dynamic, energetic

Default: 10px main dot + 3 trail dots
Trail: Opacity 0.7, 0.4, 0.2
Delay: 50ms, 100ms, 150ms

---

## **OPTION 9: Ring Only (Hollow)**
**Style**: Just outer ring, no center dot
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 ring
**Behavior**: Ring thickness changes
**Best For**: Clean, airy feel

Default: 40px ring, 2px thickness
Hover: 50px ring, 3px thickness
Click: 35px ring, 4px thickness

---

## **OPTION 10: Pulse Ring**
**Style**: Constantly pulsing ring
**Brand Colors**: #74B4D9 with opacity pulse
**Behavior**: Continuous scale animation
**Best For**: Attention-grabbing, alive feel

Default: 40px ring with pulse (0.9-1.1 scale)
Hover: 50px ring + faster pulse
Pulse: 2s ease-in-out infinite
